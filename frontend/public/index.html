Three-Stage Intelligence

Each agent is specialized. Each stage builds on the last.

Symptom Extraction

Agent 1 parses unstructured clinical text and normalizes symptoms into a structured list ready for retrieval.

Evidence Retrieval

The Retriever queries a curated medical knowledge base, surfacing the most clinically relevant documents for the symptom profile.

Diagnostic Synthesis

Agent 3 integrates retrieved evidence to produce a ranked differential diagnosis, streamed token-by-token to the clinician.

Clinical Decision Support Only. This system assists trained medical professionals and does not replace clinical judgment, physical examination, or established diagnostic protocols.
